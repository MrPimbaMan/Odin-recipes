Bread Stick Recipe

Ingredients

  • Loaf of bread cut into sticks, that is, cut into regular strips;
  • icing sugar;
  • ½ teaspoon of cinnamon (you can add more if you prefer);
  • Condensed milk;
  • Frying oil.

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil to 170°C in a frying pan, and place the bread strips, fry for 1 minute and 30 seconds, or until golden.
  2. Then place the fried bread sticks on a paper towel and let the eye be absorbed.
  3. Now sprinkle the bread sticks with as much sugar and cinnamon as you want and serve with condensed milk.
