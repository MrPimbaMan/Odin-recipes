Curry Recipe

During episode 133, our blondie teaches how to prepare a wonderful curry, follow the recipe:

Ingredients

  • 400g of meat (of your choice);
  • 150g of carrots cut into cubes;
  • 250g diced onion;
  • 300g of potato cut into cubes;
  • 2 tablespoons of oil;
  • 1 Golden Curry (sold in oriental stores), or the normal curry seasoning that you find in the market;

    • Preparation

    In a deep pan, place the oil over low to medium heat, as soon as it warms up, place the meat, saut√© it until golden. After browning the meat, put the onion and fry them too, when it is braised, remove the pan from the heat and set aside. In another pan, boil water, put the carrots and potatoes, and let them cook until they are on point (soft). Finally, when the vegetables are ready, put the meat together and stir everything until incorporated, add the Golden Curry, or the seasoning (which can be thickened with a little wheat flour and water), and stir until the broth thickens. Bon appetit!
